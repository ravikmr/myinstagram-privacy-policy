Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 16, 2025

1. Information We Collect

This application may access data provided through the Instagram Graph API and Facebook Graph API, such as:

  • Media (photos, videos, captions)
  • Basic Instagram profile information (username, account ID)
  • Associated Facebook Page data

We do not collect or store login credentials.

2. How We Use the Information

- To enable automated posting of photos and videos to Instagram.
- To manage and schedule posts via integration with n8n.
- For logging and debugging purposes (optional).

We do not sell, share, or transfer data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

- The App does not permanently store Instagram or Facebook data.
- Any data retrieved is processed in real-time through n8n workflows.
- If logs are stored, they remain only within your infrastructure.

4. User Rights

- You may revoke access at any time by removing the App from your Facebook Account Settings.
- You can also disconnect the Instagram account linked to the Facebook Page.
